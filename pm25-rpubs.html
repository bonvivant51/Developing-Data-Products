Fine Particulate Matter (PM2.5) Pollution, 1999 and 2012

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency collected data on Fine Particulate Matter (PM2.5) Pollution Levels from a system of monitors in all fifty states and several territories across the U.S. under the EPA Air Quality System. This application uses data from 1999 and 2012 to present the average levels of PM2.5 for each state.

Average Levels of PM2.5 Air Pollution by State, 1999 and 2012

The chart shows that the average Air Pollution levels for most states has gone down over the timeframe of the study.
